# Money Lab

Money Lab is a collection of **transparent, scenario-based financial models**
designed to support better personal financial decision-making.

These tools are:
- educational, not prescriptive
- assumption-driven, not predictive
- focused on tradeoffs, not optimization theater

---

## Philosophy

Most financial tools try to produce an answer.

Money Lab tools are designed to help you understand:
- what assumptions matter
- how sensitive outcomes are
- where risk actually lives
- how fragile or robust a decision is

Outputs should be interpreted as **decision-support illustrations**, not advice.

---

## Current Tools

### Rent vs Buy Model
A deterministic and Monte Carlo model comparing long-run net worth outcomes
for renting versus owning under uncertainty.

Location: `rent_vs_buy/`

---

### Budget & Cash Flow Tool
A snapshot-based budgeting model focused on monthly cash flow surplus
and capacity.

Location: `budget/`

---

## What Money Lab Is Not

- Not a robo-advisor
- Not a forecasting engine
- Not a replacement for professional advice
- Not connected to bank accounts or external data sources

---

## Development Status

Money Lab is under active development.

New tools are added intentionally and sequentially.
Enhancements are driven by clarity, not feature count.
